Computer Society of Anna University is a student club functioning under the Ramanujan Computing Centre, Anna University. With the prestige of being one of the oldest technical clubs of CEG, the club aims at bringing the students together to grow through knowledge sharing and to make them realize their potential and nourish it.

We at CSAU work towards disseminating knowledge about technology. We bring together the knowledge and co-operation of peers, juniors and alumni to create an informative network through which we create awareness about the current technologies and help in upskilling. Various events has been organised as student initiatives throughout the year where various spokespersons from the industry have been invited to shed light upon the students to nurture their wisdom. Cursor is our official campus magazine, which acts as a medium to spread knowledge and updated technology awareness through tech articles published by students.
